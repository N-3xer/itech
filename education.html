Common Questions

Do I need prior coding experience?

For our Full-Stack Intensive, motivated beginners are welcome—but you should complete our pre-work first. For Advanced programs, yes, prior experience is required.

What's the time commitment?

Full-time programs require 40-50 hours per week. This is intensive by design. Part-time options coming soon.

How is this different from online courses?

Live instruction, real projects, code reviews, career support, and a community of peers. Self-paced courses can't replicate that.

What if I fall behind?

You'll have daily support from instructors and teaching assistants. We want you to succeed.

Do you offer job placement?

We provide career support, portfolio reviews, and interview prep. We don't guarantee jobs, but we prepare you to get them.

Can I work while doing the program?

Our full-time programs require full-time commitment. We recommend taking a break from work if possible.
